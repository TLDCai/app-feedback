## How to Submit Feedback

We appreciate your input! To help us address your feedback efficiently, please follow these steps:

1. **Check Existing Issues**: Before creating a new report, please check the [Issues tab](../../issues) to see if someone else has already reported the same problem. If you find a similar issue, feel free to add a 👍 reaction or comment with additional details.

2. **Use the Templates**: When you open a new issue, you will be prompted to select from specific templates for bug reports or feature requests. Please fill these out completely.

3. **Provide Details**: Clear details help us investigate faster. Please include:
   - Steps to reproduce the bug
   - Screenshots or screen recordings
   - Your TLDC version number (found in Settings → About)

**➡️ [Open a New Issue](../../issues/new/choose)**

## What Happens Next?

Once you submit an issue:
- Our team reviews all new submissions weekly
- We'll label and prioritize issues based on impact and feasibility
- You may receive follow-up questions if we need more information
- Popular feature requests (measured by 👍 reactions) help us prioritize our roadmap

## Understanding Labels

We use labels to organize and track issues:
- `bug` - Something isn't working as intended
- `feature-request` - New functionality suggestions
- `enhancement` - Improvements to existing features
- `investigating` - We're looking into it
- `planned` - Scheduled for a future release
- `wont-fix` - Not planned (we'll explain why)

## 💬 Support & General Inquiries

- **For general "how-to" questions** or immediate technical support, please visit our [official Support Website](https://support.tldc.ai) 
- **Cannot access the application?** Please contact support directly rather than opening an issue here

## 🔒 Security Vulnerabilities

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.**

If you discover a security issue, please email security@tldc.ai with details. We take security seriously and will respond promptly.

## Community Guidelines

We're committed to maintaining a welcoming and respectful community. When participating:
- Be respectful and constructive in your feedback
- Avoid duplicate posts
- Stay on topic
- Follow GitHub's [Community Guidelines](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/github-terms/github-community-guidelines)
